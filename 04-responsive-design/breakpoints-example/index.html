Device-Based Breakpoints

Mobile Phones

  • Small: 320px - 375px
  • Medium: 376px - 425px
  • Large: 426px - 767px

Tablets

  • Small: 768px - 991px
  • Large: 992px - 1023px

Desktops

  • Small: 1024px - 1279px
  • Medium: 1280px - 1439px
  • Large: 1440px+
